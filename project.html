TYPES OF FAMILY

THEIR ARE TWO TYPES OF FAMILIES,example

  1. Nuclear Family
  2. Extended Family

NUCLEAR FAMILY

A Nuclear Family is a small family that can be made up of Two to Five people,it is made up of the Father,Mother and their Children

EXTENDED FAMILY

An Extended Family is a very large family that can be made up of more than six or more perons,it is a family that can be made up of Grand-Parents,Father,Mother,Children,Uncle,Aunty,Cousins,Nephew,Niece.etc
